Explore More Plants

🌱 1. Snake Plant (Sansevieria)
  • Light: Low to bright indirect light
  • Water: Every 2–3 weeks
  • Benefits:Air purifier, low maintenance
  • Fun Fact :Also called "Mother-in-law's tongue"
  • Ideal for:Bedrooms and offices
🍀 2. Money Plant (Pothos)
  • Light: Bright, indirect sunlight
  • Water: Once a week or when soil is dry
  • Benefits:Brings prosperity (believed), removes toxins
  • Fun Fact :Grows well in both water and soil
  • Ideal for:Hanging pots or water jars
🌸 3. Peace Lily (Spathiphyllum)
  • Light: Low to medium light
  • Water: Once a week
  • Benefits:Excellent air purifier, beautiful white flowers
  • Fun Fact :Wilts to signal it needs water — very expressive!
  • Ideal for:Living rooms or shaded corners
🌵 4. Aloe Vera
  • Light: Bright, direct sunlight
  • Water: Every 2–3 weeks (less in winter)
  • Benefits:Healing gel for skin, air purifying
  • Fun Fact :Stores water in its leaves — drought-resistant!
  • Ideal for:Kitchen windowsills or sunny spots
🌿 5. Areca Palm
  • Light: Bright, indirect sunlight
  • Water: 1–2 times a week
  • Benefits:Humidifies air naturally
  • Fun Fact :Also called “Butterfly Palm” due to frond shape
  • Ideal for:Large indoor spaces, hallways
