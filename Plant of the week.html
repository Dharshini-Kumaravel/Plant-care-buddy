🌿PLANT OF THE WEEK

Snake Plant(Dracaena trifasciata)

🌟Fun Fact:NASA included Snake Plants in its clean air study – they help filter toxins like formaldehyde!

🗓️ Featured on: June 28, 2025

General Care Tips
  • Water only when soil feels dry.
  • Keep in a spot with filtered sunlight.
  • Wipe leaves with soft cloth once in a week.
  • Don’t over fertilize: Once in a month is enough!

    • Decor Tip: “Looks great on window sills or office desks!”
